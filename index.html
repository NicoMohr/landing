What is Languo ?

Languo is a website dedicated to helping basically anyone who wants to learn a language, no matter your age nor your level. By providing lessons, exercises, tests and advice, Languo will make you want to study and learn !

Who is behind Languo ?

Hi, I'm Nicolas and I'm currently a first-year student at ESSEC Business School. I've always been passionated by languages and by meeting foreign people so I decided to motivate young people to do so and to start learning a new language.
